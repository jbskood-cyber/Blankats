# Product QA Protocol

Use this protocol every time BlankATS generates a paid CV PDF.

## Step 1: Check original input

Confirm:

- Name is present or clearly missing.
- Contact data is present or clearly missing.
- Experience, education or projects can be identified.
- The input is not empty or unreadable.

If the input is unreadable, ask for a better PDF or pasted text.

## Step 2: Check diagnosis

The diagnosis must include:

- Score.
- Problems.
- Missing sections.
- Recommendations.

The diagnosis must not use scary or exaggerated claims.

## Step 3: Check final CV accuracy

Compare the final CV against the original.

Fail if the final CV invents:

- Company.
- Role.
- Date.
- Degree.
- Certification.
- Metric.
- Contact data.

Accuracy is the most important quality category.

## Step 4: Check final PDF structure

The PDF must be:

- One column.
- Clear name at top.
- Contact below name.
- Clean section order.
- Bullet based.
- No visual clutter.

## Step 5: Check writing

Approve only if:

- Writing is stronger than original.
- Bullets are concise.
- Tone is professional.
- No spelling errors are visible.
- No awkward AI wording appears.

## Step 6: Check visual quality

Approve only if:

- Margins look balanced.
- Text does not overlap.
- The document is readable on mobile.
- The document is readable on desktop.
- PDF filename is clean.

## Step 7: Final decision

Use this decision:

- Green: deliver automatically.
- Yellow: manual review before delivery.
- Red: do not deliver, ask user for better input.

For the first real sales, use Yellow by default until at least 5 paid-style tests pass.
