# BlankATS PDF Product Spec

## Product

CV Profesional BlankATS in PDF.

## Goal

Deliver a clean, professional and honest CV that improves the user's original CV without inventing facts.

## PDF format

- Page size: A4.
- Layout: one column.
- Color: black, white and neutral gray only.
- No photo.
- No icons in the CV body.
- No complex tables.
- No charts.
- No skill bars.
- Selectable text when possible.

## Section order

1. Name.
2. Professional title.
3. Contact.
4. Professional summary.
5. Work experience.
6. Education.
7. Skills.
8. Projects, only if useful.
9. Certifications, only if present.

## Typography guide

- Name: 20 to 24 pt, bold.
- Professional title: 11 to 12 pt, semibold.
- Contact: 9 to 10 pt.
- Section headings: 10.5 to 12 pt, bold, uppercase.
- Body text: 9.5 to 10.5 pt.
- Bullets: 9.5 to 10 pt.

## Writing rules

Each bullet should follow this pattern when possible:

Action verb + responsibility + context or result.

Examples:

- Brinde atencion directa a clientes, resolviendo dudas y dando seguimiento a solicitudes de forma organizada.
- Elabore reportes operativos para apoyar el control interno y la toma de decisiones.

## Forbidden output

The final PDF must not contain:

- Fake companies.
- Fake dates.
- Fake roles.
- Fake degrees.
- Fake certifications.
- Fake metrics.
- Bracketed suggestions.
- Placeholder text.
- AI disclaimers.
- Promises about getting hired.

## Missing data rule

If data is missing, keep the final PDF clean. Mention missing data in the diagnosis, not inside the final PDF.

## Paid product quality bar

The user should feel:

- My CV is clearer.
- My CV is more professional.
- I can send this today.
- The result is worth paying for.
